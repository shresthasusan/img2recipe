Penne alla pizzaiola

Ingredients :

oil, pepper, onion, clove, tomato, cheese, basil, salt, pasta, wine, parsley


Recipe :
  1. Heat olive oil in a large dutch oven over medium-high heat.
  2. Cook and stir onion and garlic in hot oil until onion is soft and golden, 5 to 10 minutes.
  3. Stir tomatoes, tomato paste, wine, parsley, salt, and pepper into onion mixture.
  4. Bring to a simmer.
  5. Stir tomatoes and basil into onion mixture.
  6. Bring pasta to a boil.
  7. Reduce heat to medium; simmer covered, until pasta is tender yet firm to the bite, stirring occasionally, about 11 minutes.
  8. Stir cooked penne into tomato mixture and cook 5 minutes.
