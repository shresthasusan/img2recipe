Grilled chicken kebabs

Ingredients :

pepper, oil, onion, clove, salt, chicken, cilantro, cumin, chili, paprika


Recipe :
  1. In a large bowl, combine the first 9 ingredients.
  2. Add chicken and toss to coat.
  3. Cover and refrigerate for at least 1 hour.
  4. Thread chicken onto skewers.
  5. Grill over medium heat, turning occasionally, until chicken is cooked through, about 10 minutes.
